Why are the prices the way they are?

We are a local business and we try to keep our prices as affordable as we can but while hiring employees, shipping, and keeping our gardens there's a lot of upkeep costs

Are you hiring?

We are often hiring employees, if we are ever hiring we'll post it on the front page of the website, along with posters inside our shop. To get an application you can ask at the front desk or print one yourself from the pdf we'll post whenever hiring.

Are you going to or selling [this] plant?

We have every plant for offer on our site in the shop page. You can search for specific plants, and sort by various queries. The person at the front desk will usually know if we offer a specific plant. We won't sell plants if it'll cost too much.

My plant didn't grow well, can I have a refund?

Check our refund policy, theres a summary on the front page. You can submit a refund form on the front desk of our store. Certain plants which were damaged or harmed by us before sale are marked with a sticker and will always be offered a complete refund it you decide to purchase those specific plants.

Do you have any future plans for expansion into other cities?

We've considered expanding in the past but ultimately decided against it. We don't have any current plans for expansion.

How is my payment handled?

We handle all of our business transactions securely through a trusted third party e-salesman. Security is one of our most important priority's on our site.

Can I refund an online order before I recieve it?

You can refund any orders if you refund it before it's shipped. We'll send you a confirmation email before you make an order for security. We typically ship purchases a the day after you order and will send another email whenever that may be. If your order was delayed or lost, we'll send another email.
